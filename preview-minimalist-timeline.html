How It Works

4 Simple Steps to Secure Trading

Experience the most secure and transparent trading process

Agree

Everyone AGREES to the terms. Buyer, seller and agent agree to the transaction terms.

Secure

Money is SECURED in Trust. Verified buyers commit to the transaction by paying into our secured escrow account.

Deliver

Seller DELIVERS to Buyer. Verified seller arranges delivery of products or services as agreed.

Pay

WHEN APPROVED, Money is PAID to Seller. Tranzio releases funds once approved by the buyer.

Average completion: 24-48 hours
